50l Aquarium

The 50l Aquarium is fresh water with a temp of about 24°c and is primarily for the females.

200l

The 200l Aquarium is fresh water with a temp of about 25°c. It has an assortment of fish, all of which are tropical, there are Angel fish, Guppies, a silver shark and a Beta fish

Main Fish

The main fish in both tanks are Guppies.

Pregnancy

There are currently 2 fish that are pregnant. Both the fish are Guppies and are Panda and Flash.

Beta Fish

We currently only have a female Beta fish, as male Beta's are more likely to be aggressive towards other fish

Cleaning Fish

We currently have 2 fish that are for cleaning. Pineapple is in the 50l Aquarium and is a Bristlenose catfish and Lenny is in the 200l Aquarium and is a silver shark
